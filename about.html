Our Story

Iron Pulse was founded in 2026 with a simple belief: fitness should be accessible, empowering, and enjoyable. What started as a small gym in Dubai has now grown into a premium training facility trusted by many members. We combine latest equipment with a supportive community to help every individual unlock their full potential.
